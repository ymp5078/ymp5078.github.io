## About Me

I am currently an Applied Scientist at Mistral AI. I am also pursuing a Ph.D. in Informatics at the Penn State College of IST under the supervision of Prof. James Z. Wang, where my research focuses on Computer Vision and Multi-Modality Learning. Prior to Mistral, I gained industry experience as a Research Intern at Dolby (Audio-Visual Reconstruction) and an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon (Last Mile applications). I hold a B.S. in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Statistics from Penn State (Class of 2021).


## Research Interests

- **Computer Vision:** robustness improvement, video understanding, medical image analysis
- **Multi-Modality Learning:** cross-modal learning, vision-language model, generative model
